Community-Acquired Pneumonia (CAP)

Empiric Therapy

SeverityEmpiric TherapyDurationNotes
Outpatient, no comorbidities Amoxicillin 1 g PO TID OR Doxycycline 100 mg PO BID OR Macrolide (if local resistance <25%) 5 days -
Outpatient, comorbidities Amox/Clav 875/125 mg PO BID OR Cephalosporin + Macrolide OR Respiratory FQ 5–7 days Check allergies and local resistance
Inpatient, non-ICU Ceftriaxone 1–2 g IV q24h + Azithromycin 500 mg IV/PO day1, then 250 mg daily 5–7 days Consider respiratory FQ if contraindications
ICU Ceftriaxone + Azithromycin IV 7–10 days Add MRSA or Pseudomonas coverage if risk factors

Empiric Therapy

Risk FactorEmpiric TherapyDurationNotes
